Classic Arcade Game Clone

The goal of the game is for the Player (the sprite at the bottom on the grass) to get to the river (the blue row at the top) without colliding with the Enemies (the bugs crossing the stone rows at random speeds). The arrow keys move the Player. If the Player reaches the river without a collision, you score 100 points, which keep increasing each time you make the journey successfully. If there is a collision your score resets to 0. To restart the game, just refresh the browser window (F5 on PCs).

  • Client: Udacity
  • Date: July 2016
  • Service: Web Development

• Business card

This is not a website, but just the design of my business card. I wanted it to be original and display my true interests, which is as both a developer and designer. I wrote a short .html doc in Sublime Text (with Blackboard color scheme), with my contact information, a couple of bylines (.css and .js files, which shows in a contrasting color), a comment line (which STxt converts into crimson!), and highlighted my name with the cursor (which made it stand out from the rest of the page). Of course, the .html actually works! Then I took a high-def screenshot and imported it into the business card app.
